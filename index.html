ABOUT ME

    I am Erkin, a Civil Engineering major junior at NTU. Passionate about interacting with people and executing projects, I participated in several events and projects related to marketing.

    At the end of the semester when I was a freshman, I made a decision to transfer from NCTU to NTU. I devoted myself to the transfer entrance test when others were enjoying colorful college life. The reasons were to get access to a broader environment for learning, and also to encounter the divergent people here. "To decide, to achieve," this is one of my core beliefs.

    In my first year in NTU, I joined the NTU student association in the department of Public Relations. Not only did I aim to broaden my friend zone there, but I also took it as a way to learn to compose a sponsorship proposal. At the “91st NTU Anniversary Celebration”, we achieved 700,000 NTD sponsorships in total. Besides, I have taken the course “Marketing in Practice” offered by the NTU Career Center. Cooperating with students from different majors as well as ages, we finished a new product marketing proposal in a semester.

    Furthermore, I have been a manager of FB page, participated in “A MS Intern’s Day”, and even held an event called “A Drink For A Story” in the student group for all NTU students on my own.

    As an engineering major, I am dedicated to what I love. I, thus, spend a huge amount of time doing things that seem to be unrelated to my major. It is because of my love for interacting with people, and my love for the sense of accomplishment when achieving a goal from scratch.
